Our Story

With an extensive track record spanning over 16 years, we have earned the trust and recognition in the field of fabrication works. Throughout our journey, we have successfully completed more than 600 hand railing projects in remote locations, showcasing our commitment to excellence and reliability. Our expertise extends beyond hand railings, as we have also undertaken numerous roofing works with success. This rich history of accomplishments reflects our dedication to delivering high-quality fabrication solutions, building lasting relationships, and leaving a mark of craftsmanship in every project we undertake. At jishnufabrication, our legacy of trust and proven expertise continues to drive us forward as leaders in the fabrication industry.
