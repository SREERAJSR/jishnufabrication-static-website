Onsite & Offsite visit

"Onsite work refers to tasks performed at the physical location of the job or project, requiring a physical presence at the client's premises. In contrast, offsite work involves completing tasks remotely or at a location separate from the client's facility, often utilizing digital communication tools and technologies. The choice between onsite and offsite work depends on the nature of the job, project requirements, and the flexibility of the work environment.
